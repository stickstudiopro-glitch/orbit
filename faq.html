FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Is Orbit Shooter really free?
A: Yes! Orbit Shooter is 100% free to play. No hidden costs, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions. Just pure arcade action in your browser.
Q: Do I need to download anything?
A: No downloads required! Orbit Shooter runs entirely in your web browser using HTML5 technology. Just click "Launch Mission" and start playing instantly.
Q: What are the controls?
A: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your ship. Press SPACEBAR to fire, or use your MOUSE to aim and click to shoot. The game supports both keyboard and mouse controls for maximum flexibility.
Q: Which browsers are supported?
A: Orbit Shooter works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome or Firefox.
Q: Can I play on mobile?
A: Yes! Orbit Shooter is mobile-friendly and works on smartphones and tablets. However, for the best gaming experience with precise controls, we recommend playing on desktop.
Q: How do I win the game?
A: Orbit Shooter is an endless survival game. Your goal is to survive as many waves as possible and rack up the highest score. Each wave gets progressively harder with more enemies and faster movement.
Q: Why did the game freeze or lag?
A: If you experience lag, try closing other browser tabs or programs running in the background. Orbit Shooter is optimized for 60 FPS, but performance depends on your device's capabilities.
Q: Are there power-ups?
A: Currently, Orbit Shooter focuses on pure skill-based gameplay without power-ups. We're considering adding power-ups in future updates based on player feedback!
Q: How do I report a bug?
A: Found a bug? Please contact us at santoshbandi951@gmail.com with details about what happened and which browser you were using. We appreciate bug reports!
Q: Will there be updates?
A: Yes! We're constantly working on improving Orbit Shooter with new features, better graphics, and gameplay enhancements. Check back regularly for updates!
